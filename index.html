Meet Your Captains

Captain Fernando Ramos Sr.

Favorite catch: Redfish

With decades of experience around the Crystal River and Tampa Bay waters, Captain Fernando specializes in inshore fishing and loves putting anglers on consistent action in the flats and mangroves.

Captain Fernando Ramos Jr.

Favorite catch: Gag Grouper

Captain Fernando focuses on nearshore trips when conditions allow and enjoys chasing bigger fish and heavier fights. Great with experienced anglers and first-timers alike.

Our Purpose

We want every trip to be a great day on the water — and to keep Crystal River, Tampa Bay, and the Anclote area fishing healthy for the next generation.

Service Discounts

We proudly offer discounts for active military, veterans, law enforcement, and first responders. Just mention it when booking.

Catch & Release Friendly

Want to catch, photograph, and release? We love that. We’ll help you handle fish properly so they swim off strong.

Sustainable & Legal

We follow all Florida regulations and encourage responsible harvest — taking only what you need and respecting seasons, limits, and protected species.

Ask us about current limits, seasons, and best practices — we’ll walk you through it.
